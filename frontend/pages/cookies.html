Cookies Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026. Discover how we utilize essential cookies to secure sessions.

No Analytics Tracker Cookies

GarutVON is completely independent. We do not use Facebook Pixels, Google Analytics trackers, or cross-site advertisement cookies.

Only Essential Cookies

We set ONLY three essential, secure, first-party cookies for platform operations:

  • gv_session: An HTTPOnly secure token used to verify your logged-in dashboard session.
  • gv_csrf: A token checked against request headers to prevent Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) attacks.
  • gv_admin_session: An HTTPOnly secure cookie validating system administrator console access.
